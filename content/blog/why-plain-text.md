A few years ago I made a small, slightly obsessive decision: anything I wanted to keep for
more than a week would live in a plain text file. This is **sample content** standing in
for a real essay while the pipeline is built.

It sounds austere. In practice it has been the opposite — the tools become interchangeable
when everything is just text.

> Plain text outlives the software that reads it.

That is most of the pitch, and enough prose to check the reading column, dropcap and
blockquote styling later on.
