---
title: Charred Corn Salad
date: 2024-07-04
excerpt: Blistered sweetcorn, herbs and lime — a ten-minute side that travels well.
category: Salads
time: 15 min
yield: Serves 6
effort: Easy
sample: true
ingredients:
  - group: Salad
  - amount: 4 ears
    item: sweetcorn, kernels cut off
  - amount: 1
    item: red chilli, finely sliced
  - amount: 1 bunch
    item: coriander, roughly chopped
  - group: Dressing
  - amount: 1
    item: lime, juiced
  - amount: 2 tbsp
    item: olive oil
steps:
  - Get a dry pan smoking hot and char the corn in batches until spotted black in places.
  - Tip into a bowl and toss with the chilli, coriander, lime juice and oil while still warm.
  - Taste, adjust the lime and salt, and serve at room temperature.
---

Sample content. This one barely needs a recipe — it is mostly an excuse to char corn
hard in a hot pan and dress it while it steams.
