title: Grilled Teriyaki Skewers
date: 2026-06-06
hero: /images/recipes/grilled_teriyaki_skewers.jpg
heroAlt: a platter of skewers with grilled chicken thigh and vegetables
category: Grilling
yield: Serves 2-3
time: 2 hours
pairsWith:
  - summer-salad-2026
ingredients:
  - heading: Marinade
    items: 
      - amount: 100 g
        item: soya sauce
      - amount: 20 g
        item: dark soya sauce
      - amount: 1 tsp
        item: sesame oil
      - amount: 60 g
        item: brown sugar
      - amount: 3 cloves
        item: garlic
  - heading: Skewers
    items:
      - amount: 600 g
        item: chicken thigh
      - amount: 1
        item: bell pepper 
      - amount: 100 g 
        item: mushroom
      - amount: 1
        item: brocolli head
      - amount: 1
        item: yellow onion
steps:
  - Cut the chicken and vegetables into bite size pieces. Cut the mushrooms quite large as they will shrink a lot.
  - Whisk the sauce together and pour over the chicken. Let the chicken marinate for however long you'd like. Take out at fridge minimum 30 minutes before making the skewers.
  - Skew the meat and vegetables and grill. Remember that thigh can be quite forgiving, so don't worry about dryness.
