---
title: Miso Caramel Loaf
date: 2025-01-15
excerpt: A salted-caramel loaf cake with a spoonful of miso for savoury depth.
category: Baking
time: 75 min
yield: Makes 1 loaf
effort: Medium
sample: true
ingredients:
  - amount: 200 g
    item: plain flour
  - amount: 180 g
    item: light brown sugar
  - amount: 2 tbsp
    item: white miso paste
  - amount: 3
    item: eggs
  - amount: 150 g
    item: unsalted butter, softened
  - amount: 1 tsp
    item: baking powder
steps:
  - Heat the oven to 170°C and line a loaf tin. Beat the butter, sugar and miso until pale.
  - Add the eggs one at a time, then fold in the flour and baking powder until just combined.
  - Bake for 50–55 minutes until a skewer comes out clean. Cool before drizzling with caramel.
note: Sample content — the miso is subtle; it reads as extra caramel depth rather than anything obviously savoury.
---
