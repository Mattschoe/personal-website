---
title: Weeknight Tomato Soup
date: 2024-02-10
excerpt: A fast, pantry-friendly tomato soup that leans on roasting for depth.
category: Soups
time: 35 min
yield: Serves 4
effort: Easy
sample: true
ingredients:
  - amount: 2 tins
    item: whole peeled tomatoes
  - amount: 1
    item: yellow onion, quartered
  - amount: 4 cloves
    item: garlic, skin on
  - amount: 2 tbsp
    item: olive oil
  - amount: 1 tsp
    item: smoked paprika
  - amount: to taste
    item: salt and black pepper
steps:
  - Heat the oven to 220°C. Tip the tomatoes, onion and garlic onto a tray, toss with the oil and roast for 25 minutes until charred at the edges.
  - Squeeze the garlic from its skins and blend everything with the paprika until smooth, loosening with a splash of water if needed.
  - Warm through, season, and serve with bread for dunking.
note: Sample content — roasting the tomatoes is what separates this from a tinned-soup shortcut. Double the batch and freeze half.
