---
title: Grilled Shrimp Skewers
date: 2026-06-06
category: Grilling
hero: /images/recipes/grilled_shrimp_skewers.jpg
heroAlt: a grill showing the marinated skewers of shrimp
yield: 2-3 skewers
time: 30 minutes
effort: Easy
ingredients:
  - heading: Marinade
    items:
      - amount: 0.5 dL
        item: olive oil
      - amount: 0.25 dL
        item: citrus juice
      - amount: 1 tsp
        item: smoked paprika
      - amount: 1 tbsp
        item: salt
  - heading: Skewers
    items:
      - amount: 200 g
        item: shrimp
steps:
  - De-shell the shrimp and remove the poop tube.
  - Mix marinade ingredients and pour over shrimp.
  - Leave shrimp in marinade for max 30 minutes to avoid the citrus breaking the shrimp down too much.
  - Grill.
---
